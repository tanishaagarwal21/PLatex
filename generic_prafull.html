Prafull Billore

Prafull Billore is the owner and founder of MBA Chaiwala. He did not complete his MBA and dropped out of college, and started a tea stall business that converted into a big brand with his hard work. Not only a chaiwala, but he is also a motivational speaker and entertainer.

When did Prafull Billore decide to become a Chaiwala?

Prafull Billore early study was done in Dhar, Madhya Pradesh. After schooling, he took admission in MBA from IIM Ahmedabad. But unfortunately, he did not complete his MBA and dropped out of college in the middle. After college dropout, he started his tea stall. Prafull Billore also started his YouTube channel in 2021, where he puts life motivational videos. He has over 1.5 million subscribers on his youtube channel. Currently, there are 200+ restaurants of MBA Chaiwala in 23 states across India. And he is willing to open more and expand his business. Prafull Billore thinks tea is the only thing that everyone likes to drink. At the start, he did not have customers in his stall, then he gave tea to people for taste. The people started liking the tea and came to his stall. After that, he got famous day by day.

What is the reason for Prafull Billore becoming a Chaiwala?

After dropping out of college, Prafull Billore started a roadside tea stall. He knows the consequences and risks well, but he wants to fulfill his aspirations and start a tea business. Generally, people do not view such types of business thoughts as an entrepreneur. Prafull Billore wanted to have the success that reflected his Identity. Finally, he got famous with the name MBA Chaiwala which means ‘Mr. Billore Ahmedabad Chaiwala.’ He opened 23 branches of his MBA Chai Wala and wants to spread more.

Why is Prafull Billore MBA Chaiwala so famous?

When he started his business with a small tea stall on the road, he had only 8000 rs in his pocket. He quit his MBA without informing his parents. He faced many challenges, but his devotion to his work and passion made him successful. Having only a tiny amount in his pocket, talking to some close friends, and with the help of local people, he started a roadside tea stall and started selling tea on SG Highway.
